About us

Honorato Guzman Baquiran (HGB) College


is a private educational institution in Tumauini, Isabela. Since its foundation in 2005, the school provides quality education that builds graduates who are component active, and adaptive in navigating problems in both the local and global communities

HG Baquiran College


is recognized by the commission on Higher Education (CHED) and the Department of Education (DepEd) The school mainly offers undergraduate programs in the Teacher Education, Business Administrator, Crimininology, information Technology, and Hotel and Restaurant Management
